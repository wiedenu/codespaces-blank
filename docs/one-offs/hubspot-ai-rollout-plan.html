HubSpot AI (Breeze) Rollout Plan

We turn on the safe layer first, use it, and build the governance from real use rather than predicting it in a vacuum. HubSpot AI has been off for two-plus years, so the fastest way to answer our open questions is to start using the lowest-risk capability and learn from it.

Owner: John Wiedenheft, Marketing OpsReviewed with: RiskStatus: Ready for Phase 0

Guiding principles

Phased and reversible

Start with the no-cost, no-credit, lowest-risk capability. Nothing customer-facing turns on automatically.

Learn by doing

We cannot govern a tool no one has used in two years. The real questions and answers surface from use, not before it.

Guardrails scale with capability

Light touch on the embedded Assistant. Tighter controls as we add credit-based tools and anything touching external data.

$0 to start

Phase 1 uses no credits and no new spend. Any cost is gated to a measured pilot, consistent with our FY27 direction.

Guardrails that apply from Day 1

Non-negotiable, every phase

These hold regardless of which phase we are in. They are light on purpose. We are a bank now, so they exist to keep us safe without slowing adoption.

  • Training first. No AI features until the course is complete.
  • Human in the loop. A person reviews anything before it goes to a customer.
  • Protect data. No sensitive, confidential, or customer data goes into prompts without review.
  • Brand holds. AI output meets our brand voice and standards, same as anything else we publish.
  • Privacy and opt-out respected. Existing privacy and opt-out rules apply to anything AI touches.
