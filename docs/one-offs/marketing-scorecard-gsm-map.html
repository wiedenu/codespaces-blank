Goals → Signals → Measures Map

The five scorecard priorities, each recast as one Goal, a few Signals that tell us it's working, and the Measures under each. Goals are the top-level scorecard; everything rolls up. FY27 is a benchmark year, so this is about defining and baselining what to watch, not hitting targets yet.

Goal

The strategic objective (the scorecard priority). Tied to an enterprise goal.

Signal

How we'd know we're making progress. Observable, usually leading.

Measure

The specific number on the dashboard for each signal.

Your active work is already the measurement backbone

The scorecard doesn't ask for new projects. It asks for measures that your in-flight work produces. Name these connections and you own the "how do we know it's working" layer.

Marketing Request Form analysis
→ Priority 1 (requests-down = alignment, the scorecard's own success definition) and Priority 2 (JOMO / reduce low-value activity).
Activity Sync with Cody
→ The keystone. It's the plumbing behind every dashed-dot measure: pipeline contribution and lead-to-opportunity conversion in P1, P2, and end-to-end lead tracking in P3.
MarTech Audit (MKB-6270) + Data Quality tasks
→ Priority 3 assessment and data-quality KPIs, nearly one-to-one.
Breeze AI rollout
→ Priority 3 (tools) and Priority 2 (efficiency / doing more with less).
AEO / GEO content strategy
→ Priority 4 (AI-driven search, brand visibility).

The hierarchy: your top measures

  1. Qualified lead volume (SAL) — the headline lead-gen number (P1, P2)
  2. Lead-to-opportunity conversion — are leads good, not just plentiful (P1, P2)
  3. Marketing-sourced / influenced pipeline contribution — the revenue tie (P1)
  4. Brand visibility / qualified web traffic — the top-of-funnel driver (P4)
  5. Data quality & system integration health — the enabler everything else depends on (P3)

P3 (tech/data) and P5 (team) are enablers that make the top three trustworthy. P4 feeds the funnel. The three revenue-facing measures are what leadership will actually judge Marketing on, and two of them (conversion, pipeline) unlock the moment the Salesforce sync lands.
