HubSpot Naming SOP
FY27  |  Campaign & Asset Naming Standards
Campaign Naming
Asset Naming
Use campaigns only when an initiative includes multiple marketing assets and requires performance tracking. Do not create campaigns for event attendance or one-off activities.
Format:
FY## – [BU] – [Program]
Examples:
FY27 – SMG – Tradeshows
FY27 – CTG – Tradeshows
FY27 – OEG – Tradeshows
Format:
[BU] – [Program / Event / Audience] – [Descriptor]
Examples:
SMG – CEDIA Expo 2026 – Pre-Event
SMG – CEDIA Expo 2026 – Post-Event Follow-Up
SMG – Yanmar – May 2026
CTG – Marketing Momentum – May 2026
OEG – Cannata Report – 2026 – 01
Descriptors  (Use Only These)
Time
Month YYYY
(e.g., May 2026)
Sequence
01, 02, 03
Event Stage
Pre-Event
On-Site
Post-Event
Follow-Up
Purpose
Announcement
Promotion
Reminder
Invitation
Rules  (Non-Negotiable)
! Do not include asset type (Email, Form, etc.)
! No free-form descriptors
! Use one consistent event name
! Do not mix formats (e.g., May 2026 only — not 05/2026)
Social Posts
Do not rename social posts. Assign to the correct campaign only.
