# Write it down
> Whether it’s a card to someone else, or a note to yourself about what you’re grateful for, you can just write something down.

**Much like the studies that show handwritten notes** make it easier to remember the topic at hand, it seems like writing down gratitude makes you more grateful.

> Does the lack of writing it down reverse the trend? 

**In other words, does the lack of writing down your gratitude not only negate any positive effects** , but does it lower your gratitude below where it was previously? It would seem to be that both actions, writing it down or not writing it down, are self-fulfilling prophecies. 

> The days that I write down inquiries like these are the days I feel better.
> 
> The days that I send Thank You notes to gift-givers are the days I feel better.

_I will make it a habit to write my gratitude down not only for myself, but for all others I interact with regularly._
