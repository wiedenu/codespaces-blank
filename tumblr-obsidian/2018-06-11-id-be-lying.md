# I’d be lying...
## **Having a really hard time today.**

**Having a really hard time focusing.** Just wanting to be here. I’m not going to say that I’m suicidal, but it just would be so much better to be in bed all day today. It would be better if we didn’t have so much pressure on us. I just can’t think of any way right now that things are going to get better.  


I don’t want to say that the spate of recent celebrity suicides is affecting me right now.

I don’t want to say that the probable overdose death of a former co-worker is affecting me.

**But I would be lying.**

I would be lying if I said all of that didn’t affect me.

I would be lying if I said that when I read the news about Kate Spade and Anthony Bourdain and said that I couldn’t relate.

I would be lying if I said that I couldn’t understand why someone ODs.*

Is writing helping right now? Yes, it’s somewhat a relief to be able to write this out. To be able to get my thoughts out on paper.

* * *

_*Not that I can directly relate to OD, but the reasons for getting there, I can understand._
