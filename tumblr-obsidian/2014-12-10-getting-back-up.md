# Getting Back Up
> “Do not judge me by my successes. Judge me by how many times I fell down and got back up again.” - Nelson Mandela

This quote stuck with me because of the “getting back up” part of the quote. I saw it the other day, and that was what stuck in my mind. I’m having a hard time when I’m having a bad day to think that I can get back up.

**It’s been tough the last few days,** really for about the last 4 or 5. Yesterday was better than most of the previous days, but again had a tough night last night.

**Baby steps.** Or as the the subtitle of this tumblr says “daily steps towards the light.” I would do well to remember that it is going to be a daily struggle sometimes.
