# Decisions
Without getting into too many specifics (in order to keep things a little more private), I’ve made the preliminary decision to run for a state office in 2018. 

How did I come to this decision?

Last year, more specifically, last summer, I posited the idea of running for State Representative to my wife. She fully supported the idea, but after some discussion, we both decided that it wasn’t the right time for our family.

Well, come 2017 and we have a President Trump, I’ve been _way more in tune_ with political happenings at the national, state, and local levels. Today, however, the state of Iowa passed a bill on almost party lines gutting collective bargaining for 180,000+ public employees. Absolutely unacceptable.

So, I’ve made the decision, “No more.” I will not sit idly by and let others destroy what my parents and those before them have built with their work over the last 40 years.

I want my work to stand for 40+ years, just like Chapter 20 collective bargaining did in the state of Iowa.

We’ll see where this takes me, my family, and our state, in the future. For now, we hold on and fight.
