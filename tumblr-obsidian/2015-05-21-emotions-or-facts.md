# Emotions or Facts
**It’s a damn shame reality gets in the way of our dreams.**

**Incredibly frustrating week this week.** How do we balance the idea of making decisions based on emotions versus making decisions based on facts and information. Where do we draw the line? What is the line? When do we follow our gut?

> I want to follow my gut right now. 

**I haven’t been this upset since my last job.** I haven’t felt this distanced, disenfranchised, disenchanted in a long time. I don’t know if this is an emotional response or if there is more reality in my response.

**Is there information to back up how I’m feeling?** My emotional response?

> So many questions and not a lot of answers. At a loss of how to describe everything. 

Feel dark.

Feel like there’s no options.

What do we do? What are our choices? 

**Do we go on as things are now?** Continue down the path we face in front of us, the path we’ve been following for years? Follow the path that continues to take away our happiness, that continues to fight our right to happiness, to well-being.

> OR do we change paths. Change paths to one that isn’t main stream. Change paths to one that gives us the life we develop, the life we want.

**Is there a way to do both?** Is there a way to have the life we want while living in our current environment? What can we do to change our current environment to better meet the needs of our dream. 

> How can we change reality to match our dream?

_(How many questions can I ask during this post?)_

**Take a look at your lives** and see what you can get rid of that doesn’t contribute to your dream. Because if it’s not contributing to your dream, it’s taking away from it. It’s taking you off your path to your dream. 

> Place in your life the stepping stones to your dream. Remove the bricks that get in your way.

**I need to spend some time** with my spouse to see where we can do this in our lives. TV, phones, material objects. Cleaning out the cruft in our basement. We have things pulling us in everywhichway. We tell ourselves, “oh I would like to do that, and this, and this, and that, and…and…and…” ad nauseum. 

> When is enough enough? When is it time to say, we have enough?

I have enough. 

We have enough. 

Get rid of the extra.

Clean the cruft.

Let’s go cruft cleaning.

Clean up my life. Clean up our lives.

**Material goods.** Basement. Kitchen. Bedroom. Digital lives. Calendar. Business. All of these things are items that we can clean up in our daily and non-daily lives. Create a life that we don’t need to escape from. That’s our goal. Why do you need to take a day off from your job? 

> Who would be the person that tells you you can take a day off? You? Or someone else?

**I want to be** in the position where I tell myself I can have the day off. I want to be in the position to tell myself that I need to work. I am controlled by the Other. I am controlled by the Outside. 

> The Self is limited. I am limited. We are meant to be unlimited. We are meant to be boundless.

**There is no limit,** there is no boundary to which we can create. I love when we have no boundaries. I love when I can create. I love when I can follow my passion. I love when others respect my passion. I love when others want to feed off my passion. 

> I love when others find passion of their own. 

 _Bring the Self to the fore. Bring your Self to the World. Tell the world. Tell the world you are without Limit._
