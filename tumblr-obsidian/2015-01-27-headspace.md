# Headspace
**My journey this week includes a focus on trying to be more mindful.**

This includes:

  * Journaling (this right now)
  * Yoga (usually at night when dogs are calmed down)
  * Running (first thing in the morning to tire out the dogs and focus my head)
  * Meditation (started today)



**I found myself getting worked up and anxious** about a small situation at work this morning. I even told myself, “I need to go meditate right now.” It took me a couple of hours to get up and actually do what I told myself I should do.

**I used the iOS app Headspace** to guide me through a simple 10 minute meditation session. I was amazed at how quickly my breaths became deeper, how my focus relaxed and changed throughout the session.

> At the end, I could even notice a tingling sensation in my fingers and feet as my focus returned to my body.

**Before the session, I was overwhelmed and anxious.** Now, I am relaxed, focused, and ready to tackle the rest of the day.

_I am thankful for this journey that I’m taking and thankful for everything I’m learning about myself._
