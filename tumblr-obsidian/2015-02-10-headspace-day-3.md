# Headspace, Day 3
**Took some time today to revisit the _Headspace app*._**

Meditation is great. 

**When I’m done with even ten minutes of meditation,** I feel more relaxed, better able to focus on what is important, what is not important, and just be less overwhelmed with the crazy world around me. 

**The video before today’s session** helped me visualize the daily struggle I have with becoming overwhelmed. A short two minute video talked about the thoughts in our heads being cars on the road driving by while we sit on the side of the road, only observing the traffic going by in front of us. 

> Too often, we try to stop the traffic or chase after the traffic. 

**Where does that get us** but to the point where we have more nervous, bad energy about not being able to control this traffic? 

**It’s important for me to remember** that the traffic is **always** going to be there. I am going to have different thoughts in my head, both good and bad. It’s important for me to remember, though, that **they’re just thoughts.**

> Those thoughts do not define how I react or don’t react to a situation. 

 _So very thankful to have started trying the_ Headscape app _and to bring the practice of meditation into my life._
