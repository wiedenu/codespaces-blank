# Feelings are just feelings.
**You may hurt one day, but realize that’s just a feeling.** This feeling can change from one moment to the next, one day to the next day.

> A feeling is not going to kill you.

**This feeling may feel like it can kill you,** but that will change just like the feeling will change.

_A feeling is just a feeling. They’re not set in stone._
