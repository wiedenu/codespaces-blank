# My life without sleep
**Welcome to Suckville. Population: 1. Me.**

**My therapist and my family physician** both tell me that sleep is the number 1 thing that is probably affecting/creating my depression. Lately, sleep has been more difficult to come by. One night of even 6 straight hours of sleep feels like Christmas.

> When I don’t get sleep, I’m extremely irritable.

**I’m depressed** because I feel like it can’t change. How do I get done what I need to get done? How can I function without sleep?

> I lash out at people. My happy mood is gone. My friendliness, welcoming self is gone.

Sleep is beautiful.

Life with sleep is beautiful.

_I want a beautiful life._
