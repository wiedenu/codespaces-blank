# Headscape, Part 2
_“Meditation is not about controlling thoughts, but more about letting them be.”_

**This is my second day of meditating with the iOS app “Headscape”** and I have to say it’s almost better than my caffeine power naps from college. In fact, the energy levels I have after these short ten minute sessions are just as high as those power naps I had in the Union with all of the white noise surrounding me. 

**Instead of white noise, I’m listening to the calm British** narrator of Headscape. The beginning quote of this post came from the very end of the session today. I try my best not to focus too much on the words during the meditation, but this phrase stood out to me as one to remember. 

> “Meditation is not about controlling thoughts, but more about letting them be.”

**As I look at my anger and depression,** I realize now that I’ve been spending the last 4-5 years trying to control these feelings, these thoughts. As I work on my mindfulness and actually knowing what feels I’m feeling, the idea that I should let my thoughts “just be” is an incredibly powerful idea. 

 _To let our thoughts be is to be at peace with our thoughts._
