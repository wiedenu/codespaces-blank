# Support
**When you’re depressed you want to push everyone away.**

> Much of the last few days has been spent waiting until the next time I can be alone.

**Tired of forcing myself to smile** at work, at home, with family - ready to go dissolve into a book/movie/bed/dark. 

> It’s hard to realize how much you need support in these moments.

**Even if that support is just a hug** , a warm dinner, calling from far-away, or sitting and watching a movie with you - it means the world.

_Plus, a dog and a tennis ball doesn’t hurt either._
