# Weekends

January 21, 2018

10:44 pm

  


Sitting in bed. Can’t sleep. A lot going on this weekend. First of all, I feel like I was outside my body the entire weekend, except for maybe tonight. Felt like I wasn’t myself. Tired. Despondent. Took me until late last night to realize how I was feeling. 

  


Don’t get me wrong, though, the weekend started out fantastic. Had a wonderful date night with the wife and sleeping baby. Out to a couple of new places we’d never been before. Started watching a movie, but then did the new parent thing and went to sleep early!

  


The rest of the weekend, I really don’t know. It almost doesn’t feel like it happened at all. I think some people live for the weekends, but it almost feels like I dread them. I look forward to them sometimes, but it seems like more often than not that I just want to sleep the entire weekend rather than actually doing anything. 

  


_Should that scare or bother me? I’m not sure._
