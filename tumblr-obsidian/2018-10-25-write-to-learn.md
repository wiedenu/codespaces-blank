# Write to Learn
Tonight, I’m writing to learn.   


Writing to learn about myself and my current place in this world. I can stay frustrated at where I am, what I do, who I work with, and what not. I could stay frustrated. I could be defeated. I could become depressed.

Again.

I could be anxious. I could be angry. I could not believe that things would get better.

Again.

But I would be wrong.   


I know that things will get better. I need to believe that. I need to believe that people will get along. I need to believe that we will work, together. I need to believe that we will again see our work as “we,” instead of “me.”

And if they don’t get better? I do what is right for me. To make my life better.
