# Acceptance
**Extremely tired today.**

**No other way to put it other than that.** I won’t bore you with the details. I’ve been having a hard time focusing today and keeping things in perspective. I kept telling myself, “Man, I am so tired. I am not going to get anything done today. I’m so tired.” Finally, a few minutes ago, I told myself, “I am tired. Yes, I am. However, you are more upset that you’re tired rather than being tired by being tired.“

**Does that make sense?** It took me a while to realize what I really meant.

> Being tired defeated _me_.

**The lack of energy took control.** It took control of my energy level, my emotions, and my mental capabilities. I recognize that I am tired, yes, but it doesn’t need to control me or my life.

> I am in control of my reaction to what life hands me.

**Life handed me low energy today.** I know the reasons for the low energy. And it’s easy to get upset about those reasons because they were in my control, but at the same point, they aren’t.

> You feel good one day, and the next your energy is a little lower. 

**Life has a way of balancing itself** and we need to recognize that fact. There’s nothing wrong with today being a day of less energy than the day before. One the flip side, that’s not to say that tomorrow is a better day than today because I have more energy tomorrow. 

**Today is _now_.** Whether I have low energy levels, or high energy levels, today is one more trip around the earth. One more sunrise. One more sunset. One more moonrise. One more night.

> Take advantage of the day in front of you.

**I realized before sitting down to write** this post that I still have a day ahead of me. I still have time to do something good with the day. Don’t break the promise you made to yourself yesterday when your energy levels were high. 

 _Continue through the fog so that you have a good day tomorrow and not another less-than-good day._
