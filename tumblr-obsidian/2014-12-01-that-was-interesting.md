# That was interesting
**I was getting ready to write about an awesome Thanksgiving holiday****.** But then something else happened.

> Some reason, some how, my brain came completely untied when I got home on Sunday.

**And what happened in return** was me retreating to the “safety” of a dark room, a closed door, a blanket over my head, and sleeping at 7pm.

**Of course, this leads to me waking up at 11pm** and not being able to sleep until 3am. 

> It was incredibly tough to get up this morning.

**Incredibly tough to face the day, face my spouse, face the world.** I feel like I’ve let down myself, my spouse, and everyone else. I know my better half has forgiven me for my tantrum. I know, now, they are there to help. 

> But in that moment of darkness, I lash out at anything that moves, and usually the first is my spouse.

_It’s scares me and I wish I knew more about what was going on inside my head._
