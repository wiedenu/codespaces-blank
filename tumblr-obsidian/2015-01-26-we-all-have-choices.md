# We all have choices
> “Gratitude is a choice. We can choose to be grateful even when our emotions are steeped in hurt and resentment, or we would prefer our current life circumstances to be different.” [_\- Robert A. Emmons_](http://www.vox.com/2014/11/27/7295851/gratitude-health-benefits)

**The choices I’ve made** lately certainly do **not** include gratitude. And that’s unfortunate. I am incredibly grateful for the support my spouse provides, especially through the tough times we’ve had.

> I need to continue to remember to be grateful for all the blessings in my life. 

**I need to remember** that any time I think I am being hurt in life, that it’s more than likely in my head.

**I need to remember** that the _self-doubt_ in my mind does **not** reflect my actual _self-worth_ , which is much higher than I tend to realize.

> I am loved.
> 
> I am blessed.
> 
> I am grateful.

_These are things I need to remember to tell myself regularly._
