# Down, then Up
**The spouse was down a bit last night.** While they were out, I got some flowers and started decorating the house for Christmas. Things seemed to improve quite a bit when they got home and saw all that I had done; that I was taking care of them.

> As I was driving home, I thought about being in a better mood.

**Specifically, I thought about how easy it would be for me to go home and lay down,** not do anything, and just waste the rest of the evening away. I told myself, “No, you are strong. You can fight through this. You can get through the night and help yourself and others.”

> And the funny thing was, that self-talk actually helped!

I**t was nice to have the attitude of not being overwhelmed** when I got home, and instead having the attitude that I was going to do whatever I could to help others, whether it’s the dog or my spouse.

_Something to remember._
