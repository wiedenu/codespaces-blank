> If you do not want to someday die of suicide, tell yourself now that you are on the lookout for such inclinations and that you are prepared to reject them.  _(Jennifer Michael Hecht)_

**I think this has been an important part of my therapy.** My spouse tends to ask if I ever have thoughts of hurting myself, and even just asking that question makes me less likely to do something to myself. Similar to the idea of reminding yourself about your self worth.

> Positive self-talk is a powerful thing.

**It’s pretty much that simple for me right now.** Nothing more to dive into. Just the idea, the awareness of where your mind _will_ want to go to; your mind _will_ want to travel to the deepest part it can travel.

> If that deep part is suicide, your mind will convince you it’s the right option.

**Positive self-talk _before_ those dark times helps me condition my mind** and my reaction to those dark moments. I remember telling myself, “You will have these dark times, but you will get through them.”

 _You will get through them._
