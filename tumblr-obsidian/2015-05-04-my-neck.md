# My Neck
aware  
awareness

neck/feet/shins/legs and soreness

permanent or temporary

hurt or injury

go through or stop

too much or not enough

life seems to be filled with dichotomies. I seem to encounter more and more dichotomies in my life these days; encounters that seem to increase as my mindfulness and awareness increase.

in training for my first marathon, I’ve tried to bring both mindfulness and awareness to my training. aware of how my body feels during a run. aware of how my body feels after. using my mind to push me through a tough run. 

“i can do this. i can do this. i can do this. i. can. do. this.”  


“1-2-3-1-2-3-1-2-3-1-2-3-1-2-3-1-2-3-1-2-3-1-2-3-1-2-3-1-2-3-1-2-3-1-2-3″

these are only a couple of the mantras i tell myself during my runs. when i run outside, i run without music. i can’t build a tougher mind with music. music distracts me from how my body feels, how my mind feels. knowing my goal is to run longer than a marathon, to be able to run commute 12 daily miles, i need to push my mind beyond what it thinks my body is capable of doing.

i woke up this morning, tired and pretty sure i couldn’t do another run today. as the day continues, i’m sure that i get out today.

or as i’m thinking now, maybe yoga or another form of movement would be as beneficial to my mind and body this evening.

_take the time to be aware. life is nothing without awareness._
